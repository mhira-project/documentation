Built to address the global mental health care gap

In a world where most mental health problems remain undetected, we aim to strengthen the coverage and precision of mental health evaluation.

Adapt MHIRA to the needs of your service

MHIRA allows integrating psychometric questionnaires and creating customised reports.

Support mental health care workers

This project was designed together with front-line health care workers whose primary objective it is to care for their patients.
